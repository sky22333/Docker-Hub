### Docker部署文件快传

`docker-compose.yaml`配置

```
services:
  send:
    image: registry.gitlab.com/timvisee/send
    container_name: send
    ports:
      - "1443:1443"
    environment:
      - BASE_URL=https://域名
      - MAX_FILE_SIZE=4294967296  # 4GB
      - REDIS_HOST=redis  # Redis地址
    volumes:
      - ./uploads:/uploads
    restart: always

  redis:
    image: redis:alpine
    restart: always
    volumes:
      - ./redis-data:/data
```

文档：https://gitlab.com/timvisee/send/-/blob/master/docs/docker.md
